Pepperoni Pizza

Delicious,cheesy,meaty,pepperoni pizza

This absolutely delicious, cheesy pepporini pizza recipe brought to you by those of use at Odin Recipes will leave you with a newfound pizzaria and a new legal name being "mario" with the ability to jump through pipes

  • 1 Tablespoon of Dried Yeast
  • A Pinch of Salt
  • 140ml Warm Water
  • 180g Strong Plain Flour
  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon of Dried Basil
  • Pepperoni Slices
  • 1 Tin of Chopped Tomatoes
  • 2 Tablespoons of Tomato Puree
  • Grated Cheddar or Mozzarella
  • 1 Chopped Onion
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic sliced thinly
  1. Pizza Base
  2. Mix salt and yeast together in a jug with the water. Place flour in a bowl, make a well in the centre and add the water mixture along with the oil.
  3. Use a wooden spoon to mix everything to create a wet dough. Put on floured surface and knead for 5 mins.
  4. Place in greased bowl and cover with tea towel. Leave at room temperature to rise for 30 mins (should double in size).
  5. Round into pizza base and stab with fork several times.
  6. Pizza Topping
  7. Spread tomato puree over the base. Add chopped tomatoes on top then add onion and garlic.
  8. Sprinkle over cheese and arrange Pepperoni. Season with salt and pepper and sprinkle on basil.
  9. Cooking
  10. Place on oiled baking sheets.
  11. Add topping.
  12. Cook at 200Â° for 20 mins.
